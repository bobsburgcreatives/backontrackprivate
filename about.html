Who We Are

Back on Track is led by a multidisciplinary team of healthcare and wellness professionals dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with Type 2 Diabetes.

Our Expertise

Each team member brings specialized knowledge in their field, ensuring that every participant receives comprehensive care. Our team includes an endocrinologist, diabetes nurse, psychologist, nutritionist, and community-building expert, all working together to create a personalized, impactful experience.

Our Philosophy

Physical, mental and emotional well-being are interconnected. Our program is built on the pillars of medical science, psychological support, and sustainable lifestyle changes,

aiming to empower individuals to manage their health proactively.

A Curated Holistic Approach

Our retreat offers a holistic approach specifically designed for individuals with Type 2 Diabetes (T2D). Combining personalized medical consultations, psychological support, and lifestyle interventions, this 7-day program is set in a beautiful, tranquil environment.

A Tailored Retreat for Health and Relaxation

Personalized Medical Guidance

Every participant begins their journey with a thorough online consultation with our medical and psychological team, followed by personalized assessments pre-departure, upon arrival, and post retreat follow up.

Psychological and Emotional Support

Living with diabetes can often lead to feelings of isolation or emotional distress. We provide individual and group therapy sessions to help participants manage the emotional challenges of diabetes, facilitated by our expert psychologist.

Nutrition and Cooking Workshops

Learn how to prepare Mediterranean-inspired meals tailored to the nutritional needs of individuals with diabetes. Our expert nutritionist will guide you through meal planning and interactive cooking classes, ensuring you leave with practical knowledge to support your health goals.

Daily Mindfulness and Physical Activities

We offer a variety of mindfulness practices, including meditation and gentle movement exercises, such as yoga, designed to reduce stress and promote mental clarity. These activities are complemented by physical exercises suitable for all fitness levels.
