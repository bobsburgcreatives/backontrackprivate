Back on Track - Diabetes: A Unique Approach

Expertise Meets Real Support Back on Track - Diabetes offers a unique combination of medical expertise and psychological support, specifically designed to assist individuals with Type 2 Diabetes in managing the physical, psychological, and social challenges associated with the condition.

An Interdisciplinary Approach for Better Outcomes

Our program emphasizes an interdisciplinary approach that fosters improved outcomes by viewing these aspects as interconnected and equally important components of effective health management.

A Space for Rest, Rejuvenation, and Growth

All of this is delivered in a serene environment that encourages rest, rejuvenation, and personal growth.
