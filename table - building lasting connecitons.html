Psychological and Emotional Support

Living with diabetes can often lead to feelings of isolation or emotional distress. We provide individual and group therapy sessions to help participants manage the emotional challenges of diabetes, facilitated by our expert psychologist.

Nutrition and Cooking Workshops

Learn how to prepare Mediterranean-inspired meals tailored to the nutritional needs of individuals with diabetes. Our expert nutritionist will guide you through meal planning and interactive cooking classes, ensuring you leave with practical knowledge to support your health goals.

We offer a variety of mindfulness practices, including meditation and gentle movement exercises, such as yoga, designed to reduce stress and promote mental clarity. These activities are complemented by physical exercises suitable for all fitness levels.
