7-Day Wellness Retreat

Living with T2D can feel overwhelming due to the demands of managing blood sugar, diet, and emotional stress.


Our retreat addresses all aspects of your health—physical, mental, and emotional dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with Type 2 Diabetes.

A Tailored Retreat for Health and Relaxation

    Our retreat is nestled a mere stone's throw from the pristine Platamonas Beach.
    Unwind in privacy and luxury with breathtaking mountain views, a serene outdoor swimming pool, and lush green surroundings.

    See Our Facilities and Rooms
Personalized Medical Guidance

Every participant begins their journey with a thorough online consultation with our medical and psychological team, followed by personalized assessments pre-departure, upon arrival, and post-retreat follow-up.

Psychological Support

Living with diabetes can often lead to feelings of isolation or emotional distress. Individual psychotherapy sessions with a combination of groups psychotherapeutic interventions.

Nutrition and Cooking Workshops

Learn how to prepare Mediterranean-inspired meals tailored to the nutritional needs of individuals with diabetes. Our expert nutritionist will guide you through meal planning and interactive cooking classes, ensuring you leave with practical knowledge to support your health goals.

Daily Mindfulness and Physical Activities

We offer a variety of mindfulness practices, including meditation and gentle movement exercises, such as yoga, designed to reduce stress and promote mental clarity. These activities are complemented by physical exercises suitable for all fitness levels.

Building Lasting Connections

The retreat also fosters a strong sense of community, helping participants build connections that extend beyond the retreat. You’ll join a group of like-minded individuals who understand the daily challenges of living with T2D and can support each other on the journey to better health.

Serene Natural Surroundings

Experience a tranquil escape in the breathtaking landscapes of Platamonas, where nature’s beauty supports healing and relaxation, fostering an optimal environment for well-being.
